Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Crease is an offline, single-player game. It has no accounts and no sign-in, and it does not collect or transmit personal information of its own. The only time the app talks to the network is the optional rewarded ads described below.

Your progress is stored on your device

Level progress, stars, settings, and streaks are saved locally on your device only. This data is never sent to us or to any third party, and it is removed when you uninstall the app or tap “Reset progress”.

Rewarded ads (optional)

If you choose to watch a rewarded video to top up hints or skips, ads are served by Google AdMob. To show ads, the AdMob SDK may read your device’s advertising identifier and basic device information. Ads are entirely optional — nothing in the game is ever locked behind one.

Your consent and choices

Where required, we ask for your consent before any ad is requested, and you can change that choice at any time from Settings → “Manage” under Ad privacy. You can also avoid ad data collection simply by not watching rewarded videos.

Children

Crease is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
